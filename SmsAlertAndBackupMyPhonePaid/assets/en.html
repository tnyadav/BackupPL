Aim of App

Get the Backup of Contacts, Call Logs, SMS, when phone is stolen/misplaced , IN ANY CONDITION. Also Track your Phone. Once you receive the Backup of Contacts, Call Log, SMS, on email, you can also Restore it to other phone.


This is First & Only SINGLE app in the market, where you can get the LATEST back up on Email of your Lost phone by simple SMS or when SIM is changed.

What This App can do -

* Retrieve the Backup of Contacts, Call Log, SMS, when Android mobile get lost or misplaced.
* Also You can restore the Backup to your New phone.
* When Android phone get stolen/ lost and when the new SIM is inserted in the mobile, this app starts and automatically sends SMS to stored family numbers.
* Also, after new SIM inserted, it sends the Backup of Contacts, Call Log, SMS to stored Email id.
* In case your phone is using without SIM, it still sends Backup of Call Log, Contacts & SMS to your Email id.
* You can also send the SMS from any phone - "Backup" , this app will create the New backup & Email it to you.
* Remotely Hide this app from stolen person
* Remotely wipe SD card data

How to Operate & Saved the settings before you lost phone.
Mode 1 - Phone is misplaced or near by you.
Go to Setting -
* Enable SMS Settings ON & set the Keywords below - Whenever your phone is misplaced/stolen & your phone will ring Loud by sending the SMS containing the set keywords. You can send the the SMS from any phone.

* Also you can set the Ringtone - which will ring, when you receive the SMS contain any set keywords.

* Also you can Set the duration of ringtone. The phone will ring out, when you receive the SMS contain any set keywords.

Mode 2 - Need Backup when Phone is Lost / Misplaced
Go to settings
* Enable Backup Settings ON & Set your Email id. You will receive the Backup of Contacts, Call Log, SMS on this email id.

Mode 3 - Track Your Phone
Go to settings
* Enable GPS settings ON & Set the Mobile Number. You will receive the GPS location on this set mobile number.

SMS Keywords:-

a. Get Backup on Phone Lost - Send SMS - "Backup"
b. Remotely Hide your App - Send SMS "Hide"
c. Remotely wipe your data - Send SMS "Delete" (Send SMS from the Stored no)
d. Get the Location remotely - Send SMS "GPS" to your no. & you will get reply on Stored Mobile No. with Link of GPS, which can be open in any browser.

Restore Process

a. Send SMS - "Backup" from any phone
b. You will receive emails. Email contain the Backup with zip file.
c. Download zip file to your SD card.
d. Open "Backup PL"
e. Click on "Restore"
f. Select "Zip file "
g. Browse your file name from SD card
h. Select "OK"
i. Now chose the content which you want to restore it.
